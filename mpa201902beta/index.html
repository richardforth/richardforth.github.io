About Us.

MP Auto Care was formed in its current location in 2010. Mark is a fully NVQ Level 3 qualified technican having served his Apprenticeship working for Robert Dartnell at Robert Dartnell Motors in Crowland, Lincolnshire.

With over 30 years in the motor trade, you can be sure your pride and joy is in safe hands.

Opening Times.

We are open Monday to Friday from 8:30am until 5:30pm.

We are Closed on all UK public & bank holidays.

Services we offer to you.

Vehicle servicing and routine maintenance

We can maintain your car or van, from just an oil and filter change to a major annual service in accordance with your vehicle manufacture's service maintenance schedule.

General vehicle repair and vehicle maintenance

We can carry out general maintenance and repairs to keep you and your vehicle moving along.

Timing and Cambelt replacement

We can replace your timing belt in accordance to your vehicle manufactures recommendations.

Pre-MOTs and MOT testing can be arranged.

We can offer a pre-MOT health check of your vehicle or we can take your vehicle on your behalf for the statutory yearly MOT test. (on vehicles over 3 years old.)

Brakes

We can service and repair your braking system. We can also repair and replace clutches, tyres, exhausts and batteries.

Fault diagnosis and repair

We can carry out fault diagnosis and repair to all electronic systems on your vehicle. Such as Diesel or Petrol fuel injection, ABS, EPS, EBD, ESP.

Tow bars

Towbars and cycle rack electrics can be supplied and fitted along with fault diagnosis.

Air conditioning, Climate Control & HVAC Systems

We can carry out fault diagnosis, repair, routine service and maintenance to your vehicles air conditioning/climate control and HVAC system. Both R134a and R1234yf systems.

FREE verbal or written no obligation estimates.

We are more than happy to give you an estimate on any work before it is carried out and would never carry out any additional work without getting your authorisation first.

Collect and return service

Collect and return of your vehicle from Peterborough, Crowland, Newborough, Eye or the surrounding area. The collection and return can usually be made from either your home or work address, normally available at a time convenient to you.

Courtesy vehicle.

A courtesy car is usually made available but is subject to availability and management discretion.
